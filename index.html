The Initiative

What is Youth Space 6.0?

Organized by AIESEC at the University of Ruhuna, Youth Space is dedicated to empowering students in their career development journey — raising awareness of diverse opportunities, essential skills, and strategic approaches.

Career Awareness

Explore career pathways with guidance from industry experts.

Leadership Development

Build leadership qualities that set you apart in the professional world.

Entrepreneurship

Understand the entrepreneurial mindset and how to create impact.

Future Job Market

Stay ahead by understanding emerging industries and trends.

Educational Opportunities

Discover local and overseas education paths that fit your goals.

Skill Development

Gain practical, employable skills that the industry demands today.

Event Timeline

The Youth Space Journey

Three carefully designed phases that take you from awareness to action.

01

Online Awareness Sessions

A series of virtual sessions designed to equip delegates with career insights, industry knowledge, and essential professional skills.

  • Career pathways
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Overseas education
  • Emerging industries
Goal: Increase awareness and exposure
02

OBT & Skill Development

An interactive Outcome-Based Training experience focused on leadership development, teamwork, and personal growth.

  • CV Writing
  • Interview Skills
  • LinkedIn Optimization
  • Personal Branding
Goal: Build practical employability skills
03

Educational & Career Fair

A networking platform connecting delegates with universities, organizations, and industry professionals to explore opportunities.

  • Meet industry professionals
  • Connect with universities
  • Explore internships
  • Network with organizations
Goal: Connect youth with opportunities

Ambassador Program

Become a Delegate Ambassador

As a Delegate Ambassador, you're not just promoting an event — you're opening doors for your peers and building your own leadership story.

A Connector

Bridge the gap between Youth Space and the youth who need it.

A Leader

Step up, take initiative, and inspire others around you.

A Promoter

Spread the word and help young people access real opportunities.

A Change-Maker

Every registration you drive changes someone's future.

Ambassador Responsibilities

  • Share event information widely
  • Promote through social media channels
  • Encourage delegate registrations
  • Answer basic questions about the event
  • Stay connected with coordinators
  • Support delegate engagement activities
Consistency > Numbers

How to Promote Effectively

  • Share posts and reels on social media
  • Talk to friends and classmates directly
  • Reach school & university WhatsApp groups
  • Use WhatsApp status updates
  • Share personal recommendations & stories
